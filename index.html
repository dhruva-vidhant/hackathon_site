NorCal Spring Hacks 2023

Bringing community together from coders, to engineers, to writers, and of course speedcubers. And of course, we can't forget about our fundraiser portion of the hackathon.

February 11-13 2023

About Us

We're Northern California Hacks, a non-profit dedicated to bring people together and building communities. We plan to host various activities and competitions.

Our hackathon is the first of its series, and is also the first hosted by Northern California Hacks. The goal of this hackathon is to engage a larger community into stem and literature, and helping others in the process.

Brakets:

We have designed this hackathon around a bracket system, meaning that each bracket is its own competition. We will have the following brackets:

  • Coding
  • Steam
  • Literature
  • Speed Cubing
